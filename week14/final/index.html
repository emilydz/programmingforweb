Digital Looper

So what's a looper?

A looper, or looper pedal, is an electronic device that creates instant recordings of a musical performance and plays those recordings back in real-time. This allows a musician to begin overdubbing themselves to create a vast, polyphonic soundscape based on their own performances in the room.

Here's how it works:

1. Click the new loop button to create a loop.

2. Click on one of the gray squares to start recording.

3. Click again to stop the recording.

4. Click one more time to play the recording.

5. Click again to stop the loop.

Have fun & happy looping!
